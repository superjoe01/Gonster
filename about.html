About Us

GONSTER was born from the dream of Kayla, an immigrant who arrived in the United States with nothing but $20 in her pocket and an unshakable vision. With a fire in her heart and a taste for greatness, she set out to redefine what it means to energize the soul.

After working night shifts and sleeping in her car, Kayla began experimenting with bold blends of traditional Irish stout flavors and high-powered energy drinks. Her garage became her lab, her notebook filled with flavor ratios and sketchy caffeine calculations. It was there she created her first concoction — a gritty, dark, powerful drink that woke people up and made them feel invincible.

She called it GONSTER — a fusion of the unstoppable strength of Monster and the timeless legacy of Guinness. With hustle and vision, Kayla turned her idea into a movement. A drink that stands for power, grit, and elegance. Today, GONSTER is more than a beverage — it's a badge of those who fight for their dream no matter the odds.

We honor Kayla’s journey with every can. And we honor you — the next bold visionary — with every sip.
